What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer
systems. These processes include:
Learning:Acquiring information and rules from data
Reasoning:Using rules to reach logical or approximate conclusions
Self-correction:Improving performance through iterative refinement
Scope of AI
Narrow AI (Weak AI):Task-specific systems (voice assistants, image recognition)
General AI (Strong AI):Hypothetical human-level intelligence
Super AI:Theoretical intelligence surpassing human cognition
Key Characteristics
Autonomous decision-making
Adaptability to new situations
Pattern recognition and prediction
Natural language understanding
